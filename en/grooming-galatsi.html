Service Overview

At Pawsh, you can choose from bath and blow dry, full grooming, dog haircuts, hand stripping and deshedding. If you would like a detailed breakdown of each option, visit our Services page.

For regular maintenance

Bathing, drying and coat care for dogs that need consistent cleanliness and comfort without an intensive coat correction.

For complete grooming care

Full grooming for dogs that benefit from hygiene care, brushing, coat management and a polished overall result.

For specialist coat needs

Hand stripping or deshedding when breed, coat texture and seasonal shedding require a more targeted approach.
