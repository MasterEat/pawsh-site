Frequently Asked Questions

How much does grooming cost?
Pricing depends on breed, size, coat condition and the type of service your dog needs.
How often should a dog be groomed?
Most dogs benefit from grooming every 4 to 8 weeks, though the ideal schedule depends on coat type, breed and lifestyle.
What is included in a full groom?
It usually includes a bath, drying, brushing, ear cleaning, nail trimming and, where needed, clipping or coat shaping.
What is hand stripping?
Hand stripping is a specialist technique that removes mature dead coat by hand to preserve the correct texture in wire-coated breeds.
How should I prepare my dog before the appointment?
A short walk beforehand, letting us know about allergies or sensitivities and avoiding a large meal right before the visit can all help your dog feel more comfortable.
How long does a grooming session take?
Appointments usually take between 1 and 3 hours depending on your dog’s size, coat condition and selected package.
Do I need an appointment?
Yes. Appointments help us plan enough calm, focused time for every dog.
What products do you use?
We use professional, dermatologically tested products selected according to each dog’s skin and coat needs.
Can I stay with my dog during grooming?
Yes, you may stay if it helps your dog feel more comfortable and does not create extra stress.
