Dog Grooming Services in Galatsi

This page gives you a clear overview of the grooming services available at Pawsh for dog parents looking for premium care, practical guidance and beautiful results in Galatsi.

Bath & Blow Dry

An essential grooming service for dogs that need freshness, cleanliness and proper drying without putting stress on the coat.

Best for: Short-haired and medium-coat breeds, puppies starting their grooming routine, or dogs that need maintenance between full appointments.

Benefits: Removes dirt and odours, improves coat texture and helps keep the skin balanced and clean.

Duration: around 45–75 minutes.

Book Appointment

Full Groom

Our most complete option for dogs that need strong attention to hygiene, comfort and healthy coat maintenance.

Best for: Dogs with regular grooming needs, dense coats, or pet parents who want a complete service without necessarily adding a haircut.

Benefits: Covers core grooming, brushing, hygiene care and a cleaner, healthier overall look.

Duration: around 60–120 minutes.

Book Appointment

Dog Haircut

A tailored haircut that respects your dog’s structure, coat type and daily lifestyle while keeping a natural, well-balanced finish.

Best for: Breeds such as Poodles, Maltese, Bichons and Shih Tzus, as well as dogs with continuously growing coats.

Benefits: Easier maintenance at home, fewer tangles and more comfort, especially during warmer periods.

Duration: around 90–150 minutes.

Book Appointment

Hand Stripping

A specialist technique designed to preserve the proper texture of wire-coated breeds and maintain a natural, functional finish.

Best for: Terriers, Schnauzers and other wire-coated breeds where clipping is not the ideal choice.

Benefits: Maintains natural texture, supports coat renewal and helps preserve a healthy coat structure.

Duration: around 90–180 minutes depending on coat condition.

Book Appointment

Deshedding

A focused service that removes excess undercoat for dogs that shed heavily or need coat release and better airflow.

Best for: Double-coated breeds such as Husky, Akita, Labrador and mixed breeds with heavy seasonal shedding.

Benefits: Less shedding at home, improved skin ventilation and healthier, more even new coat growth.

Duration: around 75–150 minutes.
