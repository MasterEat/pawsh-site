Meet Alexandra

The Story

Behind Pawsh is Alexandra, the person who built the studio with genuine love and intention. After a long and successful career as a chef, she made a bold life change and chose to follow what had always mattered most to her: animals.

She trained at Pet Academy by Fragkiskos Tasoulas, completing a full professional program in grooming and pet care. An equally meaningful part of her path was volunteering alongside well-known positive trainer Dimitris Grammatikakis, where she deepened her understanding of canine behaviour and respectful handling.

At Pawsh, that knowledge meets warmth, patience and a beautifully welcoming environment. This is not just a dog salon—it is a place where every dog is treated like a beloved member of the family, and where trust, comfort and joy matter just as much as the final look.
